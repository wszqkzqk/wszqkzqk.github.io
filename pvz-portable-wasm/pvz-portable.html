Community-driven re-implementation for all desktop, mobile, and web. Play instantly in your browser with no installation. No game resources are included.

To use this engine, you MUST legally purchase the original game on Steam or EA's official website.
Please provide your own main.pak and properties/ folder from your legally purchased copy of Plants vs. Zombies GOTY Edition.

Disclaimer: This strictly educational project is a community-driven engine re-implementation. It is not affiliated with, authorized, or endorsed by PopCap Games or Electronic Arts.

Your files stay in your browser and are never uploaded to any server.
